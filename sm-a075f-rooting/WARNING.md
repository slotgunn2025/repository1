# WARNING — SM-A075F Rooting Toolkit

Read carefully before doing anything.

- Rooting, bootloader unlocking, and flashing custom images can permanently "trip" Samsung Knox. Once tripped some features (Secure Folder, Samsung Pay, and certain banking apps) will not work anymore.
- Rooting may void your warranty and could render the device permanently unusable (bricked) if incorrect files are used.
- Always use firmware files that exactly match your device model (SM-A075F) and your CSC/region (INS). Flashing mismatched firmware is the most common cause of bricked devices.
- Back up all data (photos, contacts, app data). Unlocking OEM or flashing may factory-reset the device.
- If you are not comfortable with these risks, stop and seek assistance from an experienced person.

Emergency recovery
- If the device is soft-bricked (bootloop), reflash the full stock firmware (BL, AP, CP, HOME_CSC) using Odin on Windows.
- Keep copies of stock firmware in this repo's firmware/ folder so you can restore the device.

Privacy
- Do not commit or publish IMEI, serial numbers, unlock codes, or other personally identifying information. If you share screenshots, redact IMEI if you prefer.
